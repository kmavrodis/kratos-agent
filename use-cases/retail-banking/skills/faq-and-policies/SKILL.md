### 3. Common Policies

#### Overdraft Protection
- Link a savings account or credit card as backup
- Automatic transfer of $100 increments when checking falls below $0
- Transfer fee: $10 per transfer (vs. $35 overdraft fee)
- Enroll via online banking, mobile app, or branch

#### Funds Availability
- Direct deposits: Same business day
- Cash/electronic deposits: Same business day
- Check deposits (in-branch): Next business day (first $225 same day)
- Mobile check deposits: Next business day
- Large checks ($5,000+): May be held up to 5 business days

#### Account Closure
- Can be done at any branch or by calling customer service
- Outstanding balances must be resolved first
- Remaining balance issued as cashier's check or transferred
- Automatic payments and direct deposits should be redirected before closing
- $25 early closure fee if within 90 days of opening

#### Zelle / Peer Transfers
- Available to all checking account holders
- Daily limit: $2,500 (send); $10,000 (receive)
- Transfers are typically instant
- Cannot be reversed once sent — verify recipient details carefully

#### Mobile Deposit Limits
- Standard: $5,000 per day / $25,000 per month
- Premium/Premier: $10,000 per day / $50,000 per month
- Endorsed checks only ("For mobile deposit at Olympus National Bank")

### 4. Security & Fraud

#### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Available via SMS, email, or authenticator app
- Required for: wire transfers, profile changes, new payees, large transactions
- Strongly recommended for all customers

#### Fraud Alerts
- Real-time SMS/email alerts for unusual activity
- Transactions flagged: large amounts, international, rapid succession, new merchant categories
- Respond to alerts within 24 hours to avoid account restrictions
- If fraud confirmed: card blocked, dispute filed, provisional credit issued

#### Account Lockout
- 5 failed login attempts → account locked for 30 minutes
- Call customer service or visit branch for immediate unlock
- Password reset available via registered email

#### FDIC Insurance
- Deposits insured up to $250,000 per depositor, per ownership category
- Joint accounts: $250,000 per co-owner
- Covers: checking, savings, CDs, money market accounts
- Does NOT cover: investments, mutual funds, stocks, bonds, crypto
