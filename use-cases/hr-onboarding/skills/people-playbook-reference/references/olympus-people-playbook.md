# Olympus Industries — People Playbook

> Version 2026.2 — effective 1 April 2026. Owner: Office of the Chief People Officer (Beatrix Holloway, EMP-1030). Reviewed quarterly.

This playbook governs people operations at Olympus Industries. It is binding on the People team. Cite section numbers when answering policy questions ("is this allowed?", "what's our cadence?", "what's the rule for X?").

## §1. Onboarding cadence

The onboarding clock starts the day the offer is countersigned. Day 0 is the joiner's first working day.

| Phase | Window | Owner | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-hire | day -14 → day -8 | People + IT | Workday Pre-Hire record (§1.1), background-check kickoff, IT REQ for kit (§1.2) |
| Provisioning | day -7 → day -3 | IT (Wesley Park's group) | Laptop shipped, M365 mailbox provisioned (§1.3), Entra access groups assigned |
| Welcome | day -2 → day -1 | Hiring manager + People | Welcome email sent by hiring manager (§1.4), day-1 1:1 booked, buddy assigned |
| Week 1 | day 0 → day 5 | Hiring manager | Day-1 1:1, env setup completed, buddy-pairing on a real ticket / story |
| Probation review | day 30 / day 60 / day 90 | Hiring manager + People | §2 probation check-ins |

**§1.1. Workday Pre-Hire record** must include: position id, hire date (Monday by default), starting salary band, equity grant terms (per §3), assigned manager. Created by HR specialist; equity terms confirmed against §3 by Total Rewards before record is saved.

**§1.2. IT-provisioning ticket.** A REQ is opened in ServiceNow under group `IT Provisioning` with the joiner's manager as `caller_id` (the joiner has no account yet). The People specialist tracks the REQ to Resolved before day -3.

**§1.3. M365 mailbox provisioning** should land 5 business days before start. If the REQ is still Open on day -5, escalate to Wesley Park (EMP-1031) or the on-shift IT Service Desk lead.

**§1.4. Welcome email cadence:**
- Day -2: Hiring manager sends a personal welcome (warm, includes the buddy's name).
- Day -1: People sends the formal welcome (login URL, payroll, benefits enrolment window — see §4 + §8).

**§1.5. Day-1 1:1** must be booked on the manager's calendar before day -3. 30 min minimum, video by default.

## §2. Probation period

**§2.1. Standard probation is 90 days** for new hires (Regular, full-time). Contract / contractor hires are not subject to probation but to contract-termination terms.

**§2.2. Probation review checkpoints**: day 30 (manager 1:1, light-touch), day 60 (manager + skip-level), day 90 (formal review with People sign-off + decision).

**§2.3. Exit criteria for confirming the hire at day 90** (any 2 of 3 sufficient):
- Manager confidence rating ≥ 4/5
- One delivered piece of substantive work (PR shipped, ticket closed, account brief, sale moved forward)
- Positive peer / buddy feedback

**§2.4. Termination during probation** requires People sign-off + a documented reason. 14 days' notice (or pay in lieu) for US joiners; per local employment law for non-US joiners.

## §3. Equity grants

**§3.1. Standard grant** is per band:

| Level | Grant (USD, target) | Vesting |
|---|---|---|
| IC1-IC2 | $20,000 - $40,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| IC3 (Senior) | $60,000 - $120,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| IC4 (Staff) | $140,000 - $220,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| IC5 (Principal) | $260,000 - $400,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| M3 (Director) | $180,000 - $280,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |
| M4 (VP) | $320,000 - $480,000 | 4-year, 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter |

**§3.2. 1-year cliff** = no vesting in the first year. Any termination before day 365 forfeits the entire grant. People specialists should never represent the grant as "yours from day 1".

**§3.3. Refresh grants** are reviewed at the §8 annual comp review for ICs at year 2+ tenure; not auto-renewed.

**§3.4. Grant size for outliers** (e.g. competing offer counter, leadership hire) requires Total Rewards + CFO co-approval. People specialist does not commit a number outside the band without escalation.

## §4. PTO accrual

**§4.1. Annual entitlement** (Regular, full-time):

| Tenure | PTO days / year |
|---|---|
| 0-2 years | 20 |
| 3-5 years | 25 |
| 6+ years | 28 |

**§4.2. Accrual model**: monthly, on the last day of the month, pro-rated for joiners.

**§4.3. Carry-over**: maximum 5 days carried into the new calendar year; anything above is paid out at salary base.

**§4.4. Bereavement / personal-emergency leave** is in addition to §4.1 (5 days / year, manager approval).

## §5. Manager 1:1 cadence

**§5.1.** Direct-report 1:1s: minimum **weekly** for the first 6 months, **fortnightly** thereafter. Manager owns the booking.

**§5.2. Skip-level 1:1s**: minimum **quarterly** for ICs, **monthly** for managers.

**§5.3. Career-development check-in**: at least **every 6 months**, separate from operational 1:1s.

## §6. Internal mobility + transfer

**§6.1. Minimum tenure** in current role before applying for an internal transfer: **12 months** (waivable by both current and target manager + People).

**§6.2. Transfer process:**
1. Internal candidate applies via Workday.
2. Current manager + target manager + People sign-off.
3. 4-week notice in current role unless current and target manager agree otherwise.

**§6.3. Compensation on transfer.** Salary is reviewed against the target role's band; equity is **not** re-granted unless level changes.

## §7. Termination + offboarding

**§7.1. Voluntary resignation.** Minimum **2 weeks' notice** (4 weeks for IC4+ / M3+). Manager + People to schedule exit interview + knowledge-transfer plan.

**§7.2. Involuntary termination.** Always with People + Legal present. Same-day access revocation in M365 + ServiceNow + GitHub + VPN.

**§7.3. Final pay + accrued PTO payout** processed in the next payroll cycle after the termination date.

## §8. Compensation review windows

**§8.1. Annual cycle** runs Q4 of each calendar year for effective date January 1.

**§8.2. Off-cycle adjustments** are allowed for: significant scope change, internal transfer to a higher band, retention case. Off-cycle requires People + Total Rewards approval.

**§8.3. Promotion windows** align with the annual cycle; mid-year promotions are exceptional and need VP+ sponsor.

## §9. Data privacy + access boundaries

**§9.1. HR specialists may see**: employee record (name, position, manager, tenure, location, status), PTO balances and requests, performance review scores (for ICs they support).

**§9.2. HR specialists may NOT see** without explicit escalation: full compensation history beyond current salary, bonus history, equity vesting schedule, exit-interview transcripts of employees they did not handle, employee health records.

**§9.3. CPO + Total Rewards lead** are the only roles with full-compensation visibility for the whole org.

**§9.4. Manager visibility**: managers see their direct reports' current salary + bonus + equity but NOT other team members'.
