# Regulatory & Compliance Reference

## Required Disclaimers

Every report must include a footer disclaimer. Adapt based on report type:

### Client Portfolio Report
> This document is for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer, solicitation, or recommendation to buy or sell any financial instrument. Past performance is not indicative of future results. All investments involve risk, including the possible loss of principal. The information herein is believed to be reliable but is not guaranteed. This report is strictly confidential and intended solely for the named recipient. Reproduction or distribution without prior written consent is prohibited.

### Market Outlook (Professional Investors)
> This document is intended for professional investors only and should not be relied upon by retail clients. The views expressed are those of the CIO Office and may change without notice. This material does not constitute investment advice and should not be used as the basis for any investment decision.

### Research Note
> This research is produced for informational purposes. Any opinions expressed are current as of the date of publication and may be subject to change. The firm and/or its employees may hold positions in securities discussed.

## Classification Labels
Use one of these in the cover bar:
- `Strictly Private & Confidential` — Client reports
- `For Professional Investors Only` — CIO/market publications
- `Internal Use Only` — Internal committee papers
- `For Discussion Purposes` — Draft proposals

## Regulatory References
Adapt to the firm's jurisdiction:
- **Switzerland:** "Regulated by FINMA. Member of the Swiss Bankers Association (SBA)."
- **EU/EEA:** "Authorized and regulated by [National Competent Authority] under MiFID II."
- **UK:** "Authorized and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)."
- **Singapore:** "Regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)."

## Data Attribution
When using external data, always attribute:
- Market data: "Source: Bloomberg / Refinitiv / SIX"
- Benchmark: "Benchmark: [index name], [provider]"
- Ratings: "Credit ratings by S&P / Moody's / Fitch as of [date]"
